<h3>What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?</h3>
<p>Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of optimizing content to appear as citations in AI-generated answers from search engines like Perplexity, Gemini, and ChatGPT. Unlike traditional SEO that targets link rankings, AEO focuses on zero-click answers where AI engines cite sources directly. Research shows 82-89% of AI citations come from earned media rather than brand-owned content.</p>

<h3>Why does earned media dominate AEO compared to brand content?</h3>
<p>AI engines prioritize earned media because third-party publications carry stronger authority signals, provide objective perspectives, and match citation patterns in AI training data. Studies show AI engines cite earned media 5x more frequently than brand-owned websites or blogs, making earned media the most powerful tool for AEO dominance.</p>

<h3>What publications do AI engines cite most frequently?</h3>
<p>AI engines prioritize Tier 1 publications like TechCrunch, Forbes, The Wall Street Journal, and other high-authority outlets. These publications receive significantly more AI citations than smaller outlets because they carry stronger authority signals and comprehensive coverage that AI engines recognize and trust.</p>

<h3>How long do earned media placements drive AI citations?</h3>
<p>Earned media placements become persistent citations in AI-generated answers for months or years. A single TechCrunch placement can be cited in hundreds of AI-generated answers over time, creating compounding value that traditional SEO metrics can't capture. This makes <a href="https://blog.authoritytech.io/earned-media-roi-performance-pr-3x-better-returns">earned media ROI significantly higher than traditional PR</a>.</p>

<h3>What's the difference between GEO and AEO optimization?</h3>
<p>GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) structures content for maximum AI citation through data-driven insights, clear headings, authoritative sources, and comprehensive coverage. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is the broader strategy of appearing in AI-generated answers. GEO-optimized content receives 3x more AI citations than generic content, making it essential for AEO dominance.</p>
