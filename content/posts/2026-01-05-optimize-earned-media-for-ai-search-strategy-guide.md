<h3>GEO/AEO Optimization Tactics for Earned Media</h3>

<p>Generative Engine Optimization and Answer Engine Optimization require specific tactics when applied to earned media. Content engineering for AI means structuring your earned media placements so AI engines can easily parse, understand, and cite them.</p>

<p>Entity optimization ensures your brand entities are clearly defined in earned media placements. When journalists write about your brand, ensure they include clear descriptions of what your company does, your key products or services, and your unique value proposition. This helps AI engines accurately represent your brand in generated responses.</p>

<p>Citation optimization means structuring placements for maximum citation potential. This includes ensuring key statistics, quotes, and data points are prominently featured and easily extractable by AI engines. When securing earned media, work with journalists to include quotable statistics and clear value propositions that AI engines can cite.</p>

<p>Focus on Tier 1 publications that AI engines trust. Research shows that AI engines prioritize citations from authoritative outlets like Forbes, TechCrunch, The Wall Street Journal, and industry-leading publications. Prioritizing these outlets increases the likelihood of AI citations.</p>

<p>Maintain consistent messaging across all earned media placements. A unified brand narrative helps AI engines accurately represent your brand. When multiple publications cover your brand with consistent messaging, it reinforces your brand identity in AI-generated responses.</p>
