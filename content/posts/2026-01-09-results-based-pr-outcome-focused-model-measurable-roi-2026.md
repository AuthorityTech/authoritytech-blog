<h2>How Results-Based PR Works</h2>

<p>The results-based PR business model is simple: you only pay when measurable outcomes are achieved. There are no retainers, no monthly fees, and no vague promises. Agencies take on the risk of securing placements and delivering outcomes before charging clients, creating perfect alignment between agency success and client results.</p>

<p>Here's how the operational flow works:</p>

<ol>
<li><strong>Identify High-Value Opportunities with Measurable Outcomes:</strong> Results-based PR platforms like AuthorityTech use AI-powered matching to identify the best media opportunities for your brand, targeting Tier 1 publications like Forbes, TechCrunch, and The Wall Street Journal. Every opportunity is evaluated for measurable outcome potential.</li>
<li><strong>Secure Placements Before Charging:</strong> The agency works to secure the placement first, taking on all the risk. You don't pay anything until a placement is confirmed and live, delivering measurable outcomes.</li>
<li><strong>Client Pays Only When Measurable Outcomes Are Achieved:</strong> Once the placement is published and generating measurable results (traffic, visibility, AI citations), you pay for that specific outcome. No upfront fees, no retainers, no wasted spend.</li>
<li><strong>Track ROI and Outcome Metrics Transparently:</strong> Every placement includes transparent tracking of outcomes: traffic, leads, AI citations, brand visibility, and real ROI. You know exactly what outcomes you're paying for and what results you're getting.</li>
</ol>

<p>For example, AuthorityTech guarantees Tier 1 placements or you pay nothing. This results-based model means agencies are incentivized to deliver measurable outcomes, not just bill hours. The agency only succeeds when you see measurable results.</p>

<h3>The Outcome-Focused Difference</h3>

<h3>Why Retainers Fail</h3>

<p>Traditional PR retainers fail because they create misaligned incentives and lack accountability. When agencies charge monthly fees regardless of outcomes, they have no motivation to prioritize measurable results. The agency gets paid whether they secure a placement or not, so there's no urgency or accountability.</p>

<p>This leads to several problems:</p>

<ul>
<li><strong>No Guarantees:</strong> Retainers don't guarantee any outcomes, so you might pay $5,000+ per month with zero measurable results.</li>
<li><strong>Misaligned Incentives:</strong> Agencies are incentivized to bill hours, not deliver outcomes, creating a conflict of interest.</li>
<li><strong>Opaque Billing:</strong> Retainer invoices often show vague line items like "strategy, outreach, and monitoring" without specific outcome results.</li>
<li><strong>No Accountability:</strong> Without guaranteed outcomes, agencies can't be held accountable for results, leading to the 70% churn rate.</li>

<p>Results-based PR eliminates all of these problems by flipping the model: agencies take the risk, clients get guaranteed outcomes.</p>
