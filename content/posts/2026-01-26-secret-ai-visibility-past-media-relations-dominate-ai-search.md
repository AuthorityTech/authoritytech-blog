<h2>Why AI Search Favors Editorial Relationships</h2>

<p>AI search engines have a fundamental bias: they favor authority and trust. When someone asks Perplexity "What are the best PR platforms?" the AI doesn't cite brand websites or paid content. It cites Forbes articles, TechCrunch coverage, and industry publications, sources that earned their credibility through years of editorial relationships.</p>

<p>This creates a fascinating paradox: the most futuristic technology, AI search, is actually nostalgic for the most traditional PR practice, media relations. The relationships that built trust decades ago are now the foundation of AI search visibility.</p>
